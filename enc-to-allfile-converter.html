Frequently Asked Questions

Can I convert an ENC file to JPG, PNG, or PDF?

Yes! Our tool is specifically designed to decrypt and restore ENC files back to their original state. Whether you want to convert ENC to JPG, ENC to PNG, or ENC to PDF, simply switch to the "Decrypt .ENC File" mode, enter your password, and download the original file.

Can I encrypt any file (PDF, Image, ZIP) into an .enc file?

Absolutely. You can convert any file format into a secure .enc file. By uploading your file and setting a password, we encrypt it using military-grade AES-256 GCM encryption, ensuring your data is fully protected.

Is it safe to encrypt my private documents here?

Yes. The entire encryption and decryption process happens locally in your web browser using JavaScript. No files are ever uploaded to our servers, ensuring 100% privacy and security.

What happens if I forget my password?

Because the encryption is done securely and locally, we do not store your passwords. If you forget the password you used to encrypt the file, the file cannot be recovered or decrypted. Please keep your passwords safe.
