Frequently Asked Questions

What is an ENC file?

An ENC file is an encrypted file that can come from various sources including WhatsApp backups, S-63 maritime electronic charts, CopySafe protected media, or generic encryption software. Our tool helps decrypt and convert these to viewable JPG images.

How do I open .enc files?

To open .enc files, you need to decrypt them first. Our converter automatically detects the encryption type and converts the file to JPG format, making it viewable on any device including iPhone and Android.

How to change ENC to JPG?

Simply drag and drop your .enc file into our converter tool. The tool will automatically extract images from the .enc file and save them as JPG. You can then download the converted images instantly.

Can I convert WhatsApp backup ENC files?

Yes! Our tool supports ENC files from WhatsApp backups. Simply upload the .enc file and if it's encrypted with a password, enter it when prompted.

Will I lose image quality when converting ENC to JPG?

No! We preserve 100% of the original image quality during conversion. There's no compression or quality loss in the ENC to JPG conversion process. All images are extracted without losing any quality.

Can I batch convert multiple ENC files at once?

Yes! Our batch image processing feature allows you to convert multiple ENC files to JPG simultaneously. Just drag and drop all your files at once and they'll all be processed together.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All file processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security for your encrypted photos.
