Common Questions

My .enc file won't convert. What should I do?

Not all .enc files contain images. Some may be encrypted data files. Try our ENC File Viewer to inspect the contents first. If you need help, email us with details about where the file came from.

Is my data safe when using the converter?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

How quickly do you respond to emails?

We typically respond within 24–48 hours on business days. For urgent issues, please include "URGENT" in your subject line and describe the problem in detail.
