Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

Welcome to enctojpgconverter.online ("we," "us," or "our"). Your privacy is our highest priority. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. Unlike traditional file converters, our tool is designed with a "Privacy-First" architecture — your files are never uploaded to a server.

1. Our Privacy Commitment

🔒 Your files belong to you. They never leave your device.

Our website uses client-side JavaScript technology to convert ENC files to JPG, JPEG, PNG, and PDF formats directly within your web browser. We do not see, access, store, or transmit your files at any point during the conversion process.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Because our conversion process is 100% local to your device, we do not:

  • Access, read, or inspect the content of your files.
  • Upload your files to any remote server, cloud service, or database.
  • Store your files or conversion results anywhere outside your device.
  • Collect personal identifiers like names, phone numbers, or physical addresses through the conversion tool.
  • Require account registration, login, or any form of user authentication.

3. How Local Conversion Works

Browser-Based Processing: When you select an .enc file, your browser uses its own processing power (CPU and RAM) to handle the decryption and conversion using the Web Crypto API and Canvas API.

No Data In Transit: Since there is no upload process, your sensitive data is never transmitted over the internet and is safe from interception.

Memory Clearance: All file data is temporarily held in your browser's RAM and is automatically released when you close the tab, navigate away, or refresh the page.

4. Information We May Collect

While we do not collect personal information through our conversion tools, our website and hosting infrastructure may automatically collect minimal, non-identifying technical data:

4.1 Web Server Logs

Our hosting provider may log standard technical data necessary for website security and performance, including:

  • IP Address: Used for security monitoring and aggregate traffic analysis. We do not use IP addresses to identify individual users.
  • Browser & Operating System: Used to ensure compatibility with our conversion scripts (User-Agent string).
  • Referral Source: The URL that referred you to our website, to understand how users discover our tools.
  • Access Timestamps: The date and time of your visit.
  • Pages Visited: Which pages on our site you accessed.

4.2 Google Analytics (if applicable)

We may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate website traffic patterns. Google Analytics collects anonymized data about page views, session duration, and general geographic location (country/city level). This data is used solely to improve our website and user experience.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Our website is served over HTTPS to ensure that the web pages and scripts you receive are authentic and have not been tampered with in transit.
