Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

Welcome to enctojpgconverter.online. Your privacy is our highest priority. Unlike traditional converters, our tool is designed with a "Privacy-First" architecture, meaning your files are never uploaded to a server.

1. Our Privacy Commitment

🔒 Your files belong to you.

We believe that your files belong to you. Our website uses client-side JavaScript technology to convert ENC files to JPG directly within your web browser. We do not see, store, or transmit your files.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Because our conversion process is 100% local to your device, we do not:

  • Access the content of your files.
  • Upload your data to any remote server or cloud.
  • Store your files in any database.
  • Collect personal identifiers like names, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

3. How Local Conversion Works

Browser-Based Processing: When you select an ENC file, your browser uses its own processing power to handle the decryption and conversion.

No Data In Transit: Since there is no upload process, your sensitive data is safe from interception during transit.

Memory Clearance: All data is temporarily held in your browser's RAM and is automatically wiped once you close the tab or refresh the page.

4. Technical Data & Analytics

To ensure our website remains functional and fast, we may collect minimal, non-identifying technical data via standard web server logs:

  • Browser & OS: To ensure compatibility with our conversion scripts.
  • Referral Source: To understand how users find our tool.
  • General Location: Basic IP-based location (Country/City level) for traffic analysis.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

No Tracking Cookies: We do not use cookies for behavioral tracking or advertising profiles.

Local Settings: We may use your browser's localStorage to save your UI preferences (such as Dark Mode) so they persist between visits.

6. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of third-party providers to keep this tool free to use:

  • Web Hosting: Our provider may log standard technical data necessary for site security.
  • Google Fonts: Used to provide a clean, readable interface.
  • Personalized Advertising: Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the internet.

7. Security of Your Files

Since your files never leave your device, the security of your files depends on the security of your own computer or mobile device. We recommend using our tool on a secure, private network.
