CRPF Constable Recruitment 2026 — Full Details

The Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) has officially announced a massive recruitment drive for the year 2026, inviting applications from eligible Indian citizens for the post of Constable (GD), Tradesman, and Driver. With 9,195 vacancies, this is one of the largest opportunities for 10th-pass candidates seeking a career in India's premier paramilitary force.

🎓 Eligibility Criteria (योग्यता)

Educational Qualification

  • Constable (GD): 10th pass from a recognized Board.
  • Constable (Driver): 10th pass + valid Heavy Motor Vehicle (HMV) Driving License.
  • Constable (Tradesman – Cook, Safai, Washer, Barber, Water Carrier): 10th pass. No ITI required.
  • Constable (Tradesman – Cobbler, Tailor, Carpenter): 10th pass + ITI in relevant trade.

Physical Standards

Category Height (Male) Height (Female) Chest (Male)
General / OBC / SC 170 cm 157 cm 80/85 cm
ST 162.5 cm 150 cm 76/81 cm

📝 Selection Process (चयन प्रक्रिया)

  1. Written Examination (CBT) — 100 marks, OMR-based objective test
  2. Physical Standard Test (PST) — Height and Chest measurement
  3. Physical Efficiency Test (PET) — 5 km race (Male: 24 min, Female: 8.30 min for 1.6 km)
  4. Trade Test — For Tradesman posts only
  5. Document Verification
  6. Medical Examination

📖 How to Apply (आवेदन कैसे करें)

  1. Visit official website: rect.crpf.gov.in
  2. Click on "New Registration" and fill in basic details.
  3. Complete the application form with educational and personal information.
  4. Upload Photo and Signature in the prescribed size (use our tool above).
  5. Pay the application fee (if applicable) and submit.
  6. Print the confirmation page for future reference.

💰 Salary & Benefits

Selected constables will receive a starting salary of ₹21,700 per month under Pay Level-3 of the 7th Central Pay Commission, along with:

  • Dearness Allowance (DA)
  • House Rent Allowance (HRA)
  • Free Medical Facilities
  • Pension under NPS
  • Canteen & Ration facilities
  • Annual Leave and LTC
