Frequently Asked Questions

Can I convert an encrypted ENC to PDF?

Yes, you can easily convert an encrypted ENC file to PDF using our tool. Simply upload the file, select 'Standard Encrypted Data', enter your decryption key, and the tool will automatically decrypt and save the content as a PDF file directly in your browser.

How do I convert a maritime ENC chart to PDF?

To convert a maritime navigational ENC chart (like S-57 format), select 'Maritime Navigational Chart' from the dropdown, upload your file, and our client-side parser will map the chart data to a PDF without requiring a password.

Why won't my CopySafe DRM file open?

Our converter handles standard AES-encrypted data and standard maritime charts. Files protected with proprietary Digital Rights Management (DRM), such as ArtistScope or CopySafe DRM files, cannot be decrypted or converted by third-party web tools because they require specific proprietary software and licensing checks. They cannot be bypassed or converted online. We save you the frustration—please use the official CopySafe viewer for those specific files.
